No Jobs

Clear labor market data for people navigating career change.

A metro-specific tool that surfaces what’s hiring near you, what it pays, what it takes to get it, and how durable the work is over a multi-year horizon.

What it does

No Jobs organizes public and industry data around your location and presents it without spin. Jobs, wages, credential requirements, and an honest read on long-term demand. You see the landscape. You decide what to do with it.

Who it’s for

People who are unemployed, underemployed, changing careers, or planning for that possibility.

Status

In development. Currently focused on a single U.S. metro area.
